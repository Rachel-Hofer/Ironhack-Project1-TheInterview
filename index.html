Job Requirements:

Two highly-qualified candidates will go head to head in an intense round of interview questions being asked by the company's CEO. Questions that are mandatory for this position will be randomly selected and presented to each Candidate. 10 seconds will be given to select an answer. If answered correctly, the Candidate will receive +1 point and the next Candidate will take a turn. If answered incorrectly, the other Candidate has the opportunity to respond (within 5 seconds) to the same question correctly. If answered correctly, Candidate will receive +2 points. The interview will come to an end when a Candidate achieves 10 points. This Candidate will be given the job offer.
