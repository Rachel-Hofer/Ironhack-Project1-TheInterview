The Interview

Job Requirements:

Two highly-qualified candidates will go head to head in an intense round of interview questions asked by the company's CEO.

  1. Each Candidate will have the opportunity to answer 10 questions.
  2. Correct answers = +1 point
  3. Candidates will alternate turns, regardless of a correct/incorrect answer.
  4. The interview will come to an end after both candidates answer their 10 questions. The Candidate with the most points will receive a job offer.
