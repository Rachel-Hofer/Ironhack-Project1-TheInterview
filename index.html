Job Requirements:

Two highly-qualified candidates will go head to head in an intense round of interview questions being asked by the company's CEO.

  1. Questions will alternate between Candidates, each with 10 seconds to answer
  2. Correct answers = +1 point
  3. If either Candidate answers incorrectly, the opponent has the opportunity to "steal" the question, giving a correct answer within 5 seconds
  4. Stolen answers = +2 point
  5. The interview will come to an end when a Candidate achieves 10 points. This Candidate will receive a job offer.
